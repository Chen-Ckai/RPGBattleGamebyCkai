Basic Rundown

- RPG with two people, each has a base sword (begins at 10 dmg)  
- Shop to benefit the players (use iron supplements as currency)

Game Layout

- Each player starts with 1000 hp, 10 iron supplements (Fe)  
  - Also has base 10 atk  
- 6 possible actions:  
  - Attack: dmg enemy  
  - Defend: equip a defence card
  - Enter shop: enter the shop (see Shop below)
  - Use card: use a card in inventory, can be used whenever  
  - Rest: automatically sacrifice a turn to gain 10 fe
  - Check stats + inventory: doesn’t take up a turn  
  
- Two phases:
  1. Set up phase

-   2 turns, any action except atk

  2. Battle phase

-   2 turns, all actions permitted

- Cycle is repeated until someone dies  
- After one cycle, all stats (atk, hp, iron) are increased (level up)  
  - Interest accrued on total money (+2 Fe per 7 Fe in storage)  

Shop
- Shop offers a selection of 7 “cards”  
  - 5 common, 2 defence  
  - Commit some amount of iron to improve chance of rare cards 
    - + money committed% of rare card
      - Ex. 12 Fe increases chance of one rare card by 12%
        - Increased chance disappears when a rare card generates
      - Above 100 Fe commit will can cause two or more increase chance
        - Ex. 120 Fe will guarantee one rare card, 20% for another
  - Reroll for 10 Fe to reroll shop offers
    - Does not interfere with initial commit
    - Increases by 2 Fe every reroll

Cards

- Cards are consumables/upgrades/equipables (depending on the effect), and can be used/equipped at any time (cards that deal dmg can only be used during battle phase)  
  - Using a card consumes a turn, but during that turn, you can use as many as you want
  - Three types:  
    - Common: consumables/upgrades with lower effects  
    - Defence: equipables purchasable in the shop with varying defence effects  
    - Rare: expensive consumables/upgrades with greater effects  
- You can only hold 10 cards at once (must discard or use a card before getting another)  
- Card ideas:  
  - Common (100%)  
  - Defence : guaranteed 1 defence card shown
  - Rare (0%)  
  - Common cards:  
    - Sharpening stone (3 Fe): increase dmg of all hits by 5   
    - Fabric softener (10 Fe): opponent’s current defence card at play’s hp is reduced by 25%  
    - Banana farm (5Fe): 10% of current iron + 10 Fe is gained
    - Rash (12 Fe): 50% chance opponent loses one turn of set up  
    - Cat sitting on your lap (13Fe): 50% chance opponent loses one turn of battle  
    - Double edged sword (15 Fe): +15 dmg at the cost of 15 hp  
    - Wall street (15Fe): increase Fe interest per 7Fe by 1 Fe 
    - Wheel of Fortune (5Fe): 25% chance for 1.5x dmg  
    - Favour (18 Fe): take one card from opponent of their choice  
    - Greedy (30 Fe): Gain a turn in both phases once
    - Yummy in my tummy (10 Fe): steal 10 hp from the opponent  
    - Robbery (10 Fe): steal 10 Fe from the opponent  
  - Defence Cards  
    - Boiling pot (5 Fe): reduce dmg taken 10%, 100hp 
    - Frying pan (5 Fe): reduce dmg taken by 50hp, 100hp
    - Strong stance (20 Fe): reduce dmg taken 50%, 150hp
    - Barrier (250 Fe): 1 battle phase, all dmg is negated
    - Last Stand (100 Fe): when equipped, hp is automatically reduced to 1, all damage from the next two cycles is completely negated  
    - Mirror force (77 Fe): Reflect 50% of dmg taken next battle turn to opponent  
    - Two Punch Man (25 Fe): one hit is completely absorbed
    - Glutton (100 Fe): for one battle phase, all dmg is converted to hp
    - You've activated my trap card! (___Fe): lorem ipsum
  - Rare cards:  
    - Oops, all 57 leaf clovers! (257 Fe): any card that uses % (chance, or anything else) based effects gain +57% (1% -> 58%, 43% -> 100%, etc)
    - Another! (100 Fe): attack once more per turn (1 -> 2, 2 -> 3, etc)  
    - Hail mary (175 Fe): 1% chance to instantly kill opponent
    - Sudden death (111 Fe): Both players set to 99hp unless their hp is already below 99 hp
    - JACKPOT!!! (777 Fe): on use, immediately award +777hp, +177 dmg, x7 iron
      - Numbers unchanged by level ups  
    - Heavenly Restriction (0 Fe): gain 5x damage. You lose all gold
    - Mutually Assured Destruction (235 Fe): +500 base dmg, but deal 400 dmg to self  
    - Go fish (225 Fe): if a card in your inventory matches one in your opponent’s, they lose that card  
    - Trolley Problem (10 fe): choose +110 Fe or +100 dmg
      - If one is picked, subtract 50% of the other from player's stats
    - Factory reset (20 Fe): all iron is converted to atk
    - Magician (100 Fe): duplicate cards can appear

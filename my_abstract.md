Basic Rundown

- RPG with two people, each has a base sword (begins at 10 dmg)  
- Shop to benefit the players (use iron supplements as currency)

Game Layout

- Each player starts with 1000 hp, 10 iron supplements (Fe)  
  - Also has base 10 atk  
- 6 possible actions:  
  - Attack: dmg enemy  
  - Defend: equip a defence card
  - Enter shop: enter the shop (see Shop below)
  - Use card: use a card in inventory, can be used whenever  
  - Discard: lose a card to free up inventory slot, no turn lost
  - Rest: automatically sacrifice a turn to gain 10 fe and %10 of total hp
  - Check stats + inventory: doesn’t take up a turn  
  - Any action that costs a turn 
  
- Two phases:
  1. Set up phase

-   2 turns, any action except atk

  2. Battle phase

-   2 turns, all actions permitted

- Cycle is repeated until someone dies  
- After one cycle, all stats (atk, max hp, iron) are increased (level up)  
  - Interest accrued on total money (+1 Fe per 5 Fe in storage)  
- After 10 cycles the following are applied:
  - Iron deficiency

Shop
- Shop offers a selection of 7 “cards”  
  - 5 common, 2 defence  
  - Commit some amount of iron to improve chance of rare cards 
    - + money committed% of rare card
      - Ex. 12 Fe increases chance of one rare card by 12%
        - Increased chance disappears when a rare card generates
      - Above 100 Fe commit will can cause two or more increase chance
        - Ex. 120 Fe will guarantee one rare card, 20% for another
  - Reroll for 10 Fe to reroll shop offers
    - Does not interfere with initial commit
    - Increases by 2 Fe every reroll

Cards

- Cards are consumables/upgrades/equipables (depending on the effect), and can be used/equipped at any time (cards that deal dmg can only be used during battle phase)  
  - Using a card consumes a turn, but during that turn, you can use as many as you want
  - Three types:  
    - Common: consumables/upgrades with lower effects  
    - Defence: equipables purchasable in the shop with varying defence effects  
    - Rare: expensive consumables/upgrades with greater effects  
- You can only hold 10 cards at once (must discard or use a card before getting another)  
- Card ideas:  
  - Common (100%)  
  - Defence : guaranteed 2 defence card shown
  - Rare (0%)  
  - Legendary (Rare chance - 20%)

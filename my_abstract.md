Basic Rundown

- RPG with two people, each has a base sword (begins at 5 dmg)  
- Shop to benefit the players (use gold as currency)

Game Layout

- Each player starts with 1000 hp, 500 gold (g)  
  - Also has base 10 atk  
- 5 possible actions:  
  - Attack: dmg enemy  
  - Enter shop: self-explanatory  
  - Use card: use a card in inventory which can be used whenever  
  - Rest: automatically sacrifice a turn to gain 10g  
  - Check stats + inventory: doesn’t take up a turn  
  
- Two phases:
  1. Set up phase

- 2 turns, only options are go to shop or rest

  2. Battle phase

- 2 turns, can attack enemy, also can go to shop if wanted  

- Cycle is repeated until someone dies  
- After one cycle, all stats (atk, hp, gold) are increased (level up)  
  - Interest accrued on total money (+1g per 3g in storage)  

- Shop offers a selection of 5 “cards”  
  - 4 rare or common, 1 defence  
  - Commit some amount of money to try and improve chances of rare cards appearing
    - + money committed% of rare card
      - Applies to every card
    - Extra chance is taken from common card chance
    - If 100g put in, commons cannot be generated
  - Reroll for 10g to reroll shop offers
    - Does not interfere with initial commit

Cards

- Cards are consumables/upgrades/equipables (depending on the effect), and can be used/equipped at any time (cards that deal dmg can only be used during battle phase)  
  - Using a card consumes a turn, but during that turn, you can use as many as you want
  - Three types:  
    - Common: consumables/upgrades with lower effects  
    - Defence: equipables purchasable in the shop with varying defence effects  
    - Rare: expensive consumables/upgrades with greater effects  
- You can only hold 10 cards at once (must discard or use a card before getting another)  
- Card ideas:  
  - Common (100%)  
  - Defence : guaranteed 1 defence card shown
  - Rare (0%)  
  - Common Utility cards:  
    - Sharpening stone (3g): increase dmg of all hits by 5  
    - Factory reset (1g): restore health by 50 and remove all status effects  
    - Fabric softener (10g): opponent’s current defence card at play’s hp is reduced by 25  
    - Banana farm (5g): 10-20% of current money is given upon use  
    - Taunt (12g): 50% chance opponent loses one turn of set up  
    - Banana peel (12g): 50% chance opponent loses one turn of battle  
    - Double edged sword (5g): +15 dmg at the cost of 25 hp  
    - Sauna (10g): apply burned status effect to player of choice  
    - Refrigerator (10g): apply frozen status effect to player of choice  
    - Wall street (15g): increase g interest per 3g by 1g  
    - Wheel of Fortune (5g): 25% chance for 1.5x dmg  
    - Favour (18g): take one card from opponent of their choice  
    - Greedy (30g): Gain a turn in both phases  
    - Yummy in my tummy (10g): steal 10 hp from the opponent  
    - Robbery (5g): steal 10g from the opponent  
  - Defence Cards  
    - Shield (5g): reduce dmg taken 10%, 1 battle phase  
    - Frying pan (10g): reduce dmg taken by 50hp, 1 battle phase  
    - Strong stance (10g): reduce dmg taken 50%, 1 battle phase  
    - Barrier (250g): 1 battle phase, all dmg is reduced by 75%  
    - All In (100g): when equipped, hp is automatically reduced to 1, all damage from the next two cycles is completely negated  
    - Mirror force (77g): Reflect 50% of dmg taken next battle turn to opponent  
    -   
  - Rare cards:  
    - Oops, all 57 leaf clovers! (257g): all chance based effects gain +57% chance (1% -> 58%, 43% -> 100%, etc)
    - Another! (100g): attack once more per turn (1 -> 2, 2 -> 3, etc)  
    - Hail mary (175g): 1% chance to instantly kill opponent  
      - Oa57LC increases chance to 5%  
    - Sudden death (111g): Both players set to 100hp unless their hp is already below 100 hp
    - JACKPOT!!! (777g): if bought, immediately award +777hp, +77 dmg, 1554 gold, deal 177 dmg to opponent  
      - Numbers unchanged by level ups  
    - Fire aspect (60g): sword now applies burn every hit (if opponent is burning, deal an extra 25 dmg)  
    - Heavenly Restriction (0g): gain 5x damage. You lose all gold as well as the ability to gain gold.  
      - One time use  
    - Mutually Assured Destruction (235g): +500 base dmg, but deal 400 dmg to self  
    - Go fish (225g): if a card in your inventory matches one in your opponent’s, they lose that card  
    - Trolley Problem (102g): choose +202g or +100 dmg
      - If one is picked, subtract the other from players' stats

Status Effects

- Burn:   
  - Deal 10 dmg per battle turn for one battle phase  
- Def down:  
  - Increase dmg from all sources by 5  
- Dmg down:  
  - Reduce enemy dmg by 10%  
- Freeze:  
  - The next hit taken deals an additional 25% dmg, enemy cannot take a turn for 2 turns
